What Lucid Does

Three layers of value from a single wallet address

01

Plain English Report

Paste your wallet → get a human-readable summary of every DeFi position. No jargon. Health factors, impermanent loss, APYs — all translated into language anyone can understand.

02

Chat That Knows Your Wallet

Ask follow-up questions grounded in your actual portfolio data. "What happens if ETH drops 30%?" — the AI answers using your real positions, not generic advice.

03

Suggestions, Not Signals

Opportunities detected automatically: rate arbitrage between protocols, idle assets earning 0%, concentration risk. Each with clear impact numbers and one-click protocol links.

How It Works

The AI never touches your numbers

01

Paste Wallet Address

Enter any Ethereum address or connect wallet read-only. No signatures, no transactions, no risk.

↓
02

Fetch All Positions

Zerion API returns every DeFi position in one call — lending, LPs, staking, wallet tokens. Enriched with yields from DeFiLlama and health factors from Aave.

↓
03

Deterministic Risk Engine

All financial math computed with decimal.js — health factors, impermanent loss, concentration risk, rate arbitrage, idle assets. The LLM never sees raw numbers.

↓
04

AI Translation Layer

Pre-computed risk assessment (JSON) → Claude or GPT translates into plain English. The AI only does language — every number it shows was calculated beforehand.

↓
05

Report + Chat + Suggestions

User gets a readable report, can ask follow-ups in chat (grounded in their data), and sees optimization suggestions ranked by potential dollar impact.

03

Architecture Principles

01

LLM Never Touches Numbers

Risk engine computes everything with decimal.js. LLM only translates pre-computed JSON → natural language. No hallucinated financial data.

02

No Financial Advice

"Opportunities detected" not "you should." Disclaimers on every report. Observations and education, never recommendations.

03

BYOK-First Billing

Users bring their own API key for unlimited free usage. Platform key available with daily limits. Power users pay only what they use.

04

Graceful Degradation

Only Zerion is critical. DeFiLlama down → skip yield comparison. Aave down → skip health factor. LLM down → show raw structured data.
